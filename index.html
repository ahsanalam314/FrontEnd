Scattered across endless lands and open oceans, time zones are problematic.

FIO alleviates those woes.

Figure it Out [FIO]

A visually stimulating chrome extension to solve your time zone pain.

Auto-location generation

If you decide to fly the coop, FIO will either add you to, or creates a new location.
24hr or AM / PM

FIO caters to all your time telling needs. Be it 24hr clocks, or AM / PM style, it does it all.
National holidays

FIO is nice enough to show you when your locations have a national holiday.
Location compression

Up to three locations that share the same time zone get grouped together. Squisheems.
